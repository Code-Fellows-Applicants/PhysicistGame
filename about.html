My name is Yoni, I recently graduated from UW, where I studied BioPhysics. In my 4 years I learned about many significant physicists, at times diving head first into system and methods that they produced themselves. I studied classical mechanics, quantum mechanics, particle physics, fluid dynamics, statistical mechanics, thermodynamics, electromagnetism, and others. These physicists were everywhere in my scientific learnings, but I could never seem to match the face to the name. So I made this game to help me remember their faces.
