Hi! My name is Yoni, welcome to my game for nerds!

I am a recent graduate from the University of Washington, and I studied BioPhysics for my undergraduate BS. In my 4 years I learned about many significant physicists, at times diving head first into system and methods that they produced themselves. I studied classical mechanics, quantum mechanics, particle physics, fluid dynamics, statistical mechanics, thermodynamics, electromagnetism, and many more. In addition to the physics, I studied microbiology, organic chemistry, biochemistry, anatomy, and many other disciplines that showed me other applications for these physical laws. These physicists were everywhere in my scientific learnings, but I could never seem to match the face to the name. So I made this game to help me remember their faces. Although I have learned about many distinct fields of science, my education was limited in the scope of Computer Science and Big data analytics, although we touched on some methods in my lab classes. That is why I hope to join code fellows and learn about CS, data analysis, and using computers to simplify problems using logic.
